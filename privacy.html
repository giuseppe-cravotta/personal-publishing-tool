Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 June 2026

Giuseppe's Personal Publishing Tool ("the App") is a personal, single-user application. The only user is its developer, who uses it to schedule and publish his own videos to his own social media and video channels (including YouTube). The App is not offered to, or used by, any third party.

What data the App accesses

To publish on the developer's behalf, the App uses official platform APIs (for example, the YouTube Data API). Authentication is handled through standard OAuth 2.0. The App receives access and refresh tokens that allow it to upload and schedule the developer's own videos to the developer's own channel.

Where data is stored

All OAuth tokens and credentials are stored locally on the developer's own personal computer (using the operating system's secure credential storage). They are never transmitted to any external server operated by the App, never shared with anyone, and never sold.

What the App does NOT do

  • It does not collect data about any other person.
  • It does not have other users, accounts, or sign-ups.
  • It does not share, sell, rent, or transfer any data to third parties.
  • It does not perform analytics, tracking, profiling, scraping, or bulk download.
  • It does not use the data for advertising.

Data retention

Tokens are kept locally only for as long as needed to operate the App and can be revoked at any time by the developer through the relevant platform's account settings.
